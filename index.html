    About Us

    Bangalore Job Seekers is a online community which was formed on 13th of April’ 2011. Here a Job Seeker can get all the necessary information required in order to search for a right Job/Needs in Bangalore. It’s not just like a job portal. It’s the playground of everything related to a Job Seeker. It is completely free for a Job Seeker to use the site. Our main areas of activity are our industry supplier directory.

    Even if you have a job, still you can’t disagree that you are not looking for better opportunities. In this way everyone, searching for a job or already working in Bangalore, are referred as Bangalore Job Seekers(BJS). It’s not just like a job portal. It’s the playground of everything related to all Job Seekers.

    • At BJS, our goal is to help everyone to find the right information about each and every companies in Bangalore. Whether you’re thinking about a new job, new career, a new city or a new direction, BJS helps you explore the possibilities and find the opportunities that are right for you. Bangalorejobseekers.com provides impartial reviews across a variety of categories to help you to make an wise decision. You can view the Listings/Events to get all of the information you need and reading reviews by our experts as well as user reviews by the BJS community.
